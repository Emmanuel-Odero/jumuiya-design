Our Mission

More Than a Game —
A Movement

Jumuiya — Swahili for "community" — embodies our core belief: chess is best experienced together. Founded in Nairobi, we have grown into a nationwide network connecting players, coaches, and enthusiasts of every background.

From beginner workshops in rural schools to FIDE-rated open championships, we celebrate every mind that dares to sit across the board.
