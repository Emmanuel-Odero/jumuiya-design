Our Mission & Vision

Building a thriving chess ecosystem rooted in community, excellence, and inclusivity across East Africa.

Jumuiya

More Than a Game — A Movement

Jumuiya — Swahili for "community" — embodies our core belief: chess is best experienced together. Founded in Nairobi, we have grown into a nationwide network connecting players, coaches, and enthusiasts.

🎯Competitive ExcellenceFIDE-rated tournaments & pathways
🌱Grassroots DevelopmentSchool & community programmes
🤝Inclusive CommunityAll ages, genders & abilities
🌍Pan-African VisionConnecting across the continent
